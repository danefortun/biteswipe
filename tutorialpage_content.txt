Pitch (for reference):
Do you find yourself wondering where you want to eat, what you want to play,
what music you want to listen to? I walk around campus as a new transfer 
and have to trial-and-error new places to eat, study, and relax. What I
think is that people should have a way to know what places they might like 
to visit and explore around campus without doing as much legwork. 
LifeSwipe is the perfect solution for that problem, so that you can know 
ahead of time what places will make your campus life great!

Welcome to LifeSwipe!

Thank you for joining us! Here, you have a place to:
- Discover new interests
- Make new connections
- Express yourself and what you love

What can I do?

- Check out recommended interests on the Cards page
[gif of clicking on Cards, then swiping through several]
1. Click on Cards in the bottom footer
2. Hover over "Filter Menu" to see a list of settings to personalize your feed
3. Swipe or click on the buttons for the recommendations you like or dislike
4. Check out My Stuff to see what you liked later

- Personalize your profile
[gif of setting up profile]
1. Click on Profile in the bottom footer
2. Upload a profile picture
    - Click "Browse", select a picture, then click "Upload"
3. Write a bio under "About Me"

- Make new friends and connect with them
[gif demoing chat features]
1. Click Chat in the bottom footer
2. Say hi to your peers and make friends!

- Go out and experience everything you've swiped on!
[gif/screenshot showing My Stuff page]
1. Click My Stuff in the bottom footer
2. See everything you've liked that caught your eye
3. Go out and make some new memories!
